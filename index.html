Delicate Arch, Utah

Delicate Arch is a natural sandstone arch located in Arches National Park in Utah, USA. Standing 60 feet tall, it is one of the most famous geological features in the park and a symbol of the American West. Its unique shape and striking position against the sky make it a popular hiking destination and a photogenic landmark, embodying the rugged beauty of the desert landscape.
